--- layout: post title: "Creating "soft-links" to folders on Linux" date: 2008-11-11 comments: false tags: [ Linux ] --- If you're working on Linux, especially on the command line, you'll often have to navigate through a lot of directories. This clearly depends on your directory structure, but it may be the case. That's however really annoying, so creating a link may easy your life:
ln -s <fileonharddisk> <name>
